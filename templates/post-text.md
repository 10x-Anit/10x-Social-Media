# Text Post Template
<!-- [F:TMPL.01] Plain text post structure -->

## Structure

```
{{HOOK}}

{{BODY_PARAGRAPH_1}}

{{BODY_PARAGRAPH_2}}

{{INSIGHT_OR_LESSON}}

{{CTA}}

{{HASHTAGS}}
```

## Field Rules

- **HOOK**: 1 line, under 100 chars, stops the scroll
- **BODY**: 2-3 short paragraphs, 1 idea each
- **INSIGHT**: The takeaway — what did you learn / what should they know
- **CTA**: Question or action ("What's your experience with X?")
- **HASHTAGS**: 3-5 for LinkedIn, 0-2 for Twitter, 5-15 for Instagram
