# Article Post Template
<!-- [F:TMPL.04] Long-form article structure -->

## Structure

```
# {{TITLE — clear benefit + keyword}}

{{INTRO — 2-3 sentences, state the problem/opportunity}}

## {{SECTION_1_HEADING}}
{{CONTENT — 150-300 words}}

## {{SECTION_2_HEADING}}
{{CONTENT — 150-300 words}}

## {{SECTION_3_HEADING}}
{{CONTENT — 150-300 words}}

## Key Takeaways
- {{TAKEAWAY_1}}
- {{TAKEAWAY_2}}
- {{TAKEAWAY_3}}

## {{CTA_SECTION}}
{{QUESTION_OR_NEXT_STEP}}
```

## Rules
- 800-2,000 words total
- Subheadings every 200-300 words
- Use bullet points and numbered lists for scannability
- Include 1-2 relevant images or diagrams
- End with a discussion question
