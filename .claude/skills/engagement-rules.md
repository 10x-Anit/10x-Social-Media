## Comment Strategy

### When to comment (via Playwright)
- On posts from people in your target audience
- On trending posts in your industry
- On posts where you can add genuine value
- Reply to every comment on YOUR posts within 24h

### Comment quality rules
- Add value — share an experience, add a data point, or ask a smart question
- Minimum 2 sentences (one-word comments look spammy)
- Never "Great post!" or "Love this!" without adding substance
- Mention specific points from the post

### Engagement cadence
- 15-30 min per day engaging with others' content
- 5-10 meaningful comments per day
- Reply to your own post comments within 2 hours of posting (algorithm boost)

## First Comment Strategy
- Post a first comment on your own posts immediately
- Use it to add context, ask a follow-up question, or share a related resource
- This boosts the post in the algorithm

## DM Rules (via Playwright)
- Never cold-pitch in DMs
- Only DM after 3+ meaningful comment interactions
- Keep it conversational, not salesy

## Engagement Don'ts
- Don't engage with controversial/political content from brand account
- Don't argue in comments
- Don't mass-like posts (looks automated)
- Don't comment on competitor posts negatively
