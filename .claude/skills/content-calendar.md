## Weekly Cadence (customize to your schedule)

| Day | Platform | Content Type | Topic Pillar |
|-----|----------|-------------|-------------|
| Mon | LinkedIn | Text post | Industry insight |
| Tue | Twitter/X | Thread | How-to / Tutorial |
| Wed | LinkedIn | Carousel | Case study / Results |
| Thu | Instagram | Visual + caption | Behind the scenes |
| Fri | LinkedIn | Poll or question | Community engagement |
| Sat | — | Rest or batch create | — |
| Sun | — | Plan next week | — |

| TikTok | Tue-Thu 7-9pm, Sat-Sun 10am | Mon morning |

## Topic Pillars (customize these)

1. **Educational** (40%) — Teach something actionable
2. **Personal/Story** (20%) — Share experiences, lessons
3. **Engagement** (20%) — Questions, polls, discussions
4. **Promotional** (10%) — Products, services, launches
5. **Curated** (10%) — Share others' content with your take

## Content Ratio Rule
Never post more than 1 promotional post for every 4 value posts.

## CUSTOMIZE THIS FILE
These are defaults. After 2-4 weeks of posting, check your analytics
([F:CMD.04]) and adjust times, frequency, and pillars based on what
actually performs for YOUR audience.
